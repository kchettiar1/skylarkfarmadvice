Skylark Farm Advice Privacy Policy

Skylark Farm Advice is committed to protecting the privacy of those who entrust us with their personal information and strive to comply with all relevant legislation with regard to Data Protection.

You can decide not to receive communications or change how we contact you at any time. If you wish to do so please email: amanda@skylarkfarmadvice.co.uk or telephone: +44 73 05 07 2073

Skylark Farm Advice will never sell your personal data, and will only ever share it with organisations we work with where necessary and if its privacy and security are guaranteed.

How we collect information

We collect information about you when you request further information about our services.

Skylark Farm Advice sometimes receives personal data about individuals from third parties. For example, if we are partnering with or delivering work on behalf of another organisation.

Sensitive personal data

Skylark Farm Advice does not normally collect or store sensitive personal data (such as information relating to health, beliefs or political affiliation). However there may be some situations where this will occur (e.g. if working with us and health information is relevant to health and safety requirements). If this does occur, extra care will be taken to ensure your privacy rights are protected.

How we use information

Skylark Farm Advice will only ever use your personal data with your consent, or where it is necessary in order to:

  • enter into, or perform, a contract with you;
  • comply with a legal duty;
  • protect your vital interests;
  • for our own (or a third party’s) lawful interests, provided your rights don’t override these.

Marketing

Skylark Farm Advice would like to send you information about other services we provide which may be of interest to you.

If you have consented to receive such information you may opt out at a later date.

If you no longer wish to be contacted for marketing purposes, please email: amanda@skylarkfarmadvice.co.uk

Disclosing and sharing data

Skylark Farm Advice will not sell your data to any third party. However, we may share your data with trusted service providers in order to fulfil the required services. We’ll only share information when necessary and for the purposes that you have provided the data to us.

Where other companies are used to capture data or undertake fulfilment services on our behalf, these activities are carried out in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulations requirements.

The Privacy Policy will apply whenever you provide personal information, except as required by law, where Skylark Farm Advice can pass on personal data to the relevant authorities, without consent e.g. for the purpose to prevent and detect crime.

Accessing and correcting information

You have the right to request a copy of the information that Skylark Farm Advice holds about you. If you would like to exercise this right, please email amanda@skylarkfarmadvice.co.uk or telephone 07305 072073 to request the postal address.

We want to make sure that your personal information is accurate and up to date. You may ask us to correct or remove information you think is inaccurate.

Cookies

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard internet log information and visitor behaviour information which is used to track visitor use of the website and to compile statistical reports on website activity.

You can set your browser not to accept cookies and the website www.aboutcookies.org explains how you can remove them from your browser.

Changes to our privacy notice

This privacy notice is kept under review and any updates will be placed on the Skylark Farm Advice website as necessary. This notice was last updated 24th April 2023.
